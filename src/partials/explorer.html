Every chapter unlocks a new city filled with its own visual theme, puzzles, and fun facts:

  • Paris: Puzzle your way through romantic gardens and Eiffel views

  • Kyoto: Solve elegant bamboo and blossom-themed boards

  • New York: Bright lights, dynamic levels, and modern tile sets

  • Cairo: Match ancient symbols in the shadows of the pyramids

  • Barcelona: Mosaic tiles and vibrant architecture challenges

Each city has multiple sub-levels, secrets to discover, and animated backdrops.
