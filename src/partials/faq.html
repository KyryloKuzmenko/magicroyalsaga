FAQ FAQ

  • Is Royal Journey free to play?

    Yes, it’s free to download and play with optional in-app purchases.

  • Do I need an internet connection to play?

    No — the game supports offline play. You can sync when back online.

  • How many levels are there?

    Over 2800+ levels, with more added regularly.

  • Are there leaderboards?

    Yes, compete globally or with friends via the in-game leaderboard.

  • What’s the Royal Pass?

    A seasonal event with exclusive rewards, boosters, and royal outfits for King William.
